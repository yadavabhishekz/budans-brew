Welcome to Budan's Brew, your urban sanctuary located in the bustling hub of Sector 62, Noida. At Budan's Brew, we believe that coffee is more than just a drink—it’s a daily ritual, a spark of inspiration, and a way to bring people together in a fast-paced city.

Our Story
Founded in 2024, Budan's Brew started as a passion project with one mission: to bring the finest Indian specialty coffee to our city, paying homage to the spirit of Baba Budan.

Our love for authentic brews led us on a journey across the misty hills of Coorg and Chikmagalur, where we formed direct partnerships with heritage estates. We ensure that every cup we brew honors the farmer's craft, from the plantation to your cup. Our beans are roasted locally to highlight the rich, bold notes characteristic of Indian terroir.

Delivery & Locations Served
In addition to offering a vibrant space to enjoy coffee in our café, we proudly deliver to Sector 18, Sector 62, the Expressway, and surrounding residential societies. Whether you’re working from a high-rise office, relaxing at home, or taking a break near the DLF Mall of India, we bring your favorite brew right to your desk or doorstep. Just a tap away via our delivery partners, our service ensures that you never miss your caffeine fix, amidst the city traffic.

Our Menu
Our menu offers something for every palate, from our signature aromatic pour-overs to creamy frappes, artisanal loose-leaf teas, and fresh pastries sourced from top local bakers. We also cater to health-conscious lifestyles with a variety of oat and almond milk options and guilt-free, healthy snacking alternatives.

Community & Sustainability
At Budan's Brew, we are more than just a café. We are a part of the Noida community, and we care deeply about our environment. We use biodegradable packaging, source ingredients locally, and strive to maintain sustainable operations. Our space frequently hosts events, such as open mic nights, book club meetups, and workshops, making it a thriving hub for conversation and creativity.
